The Pythagorean Theorem States that in a Right Triangle, \[ a^2 + b^2 = c^2\].
The Law of Sines States that in a non-right triangle, \[\frac{A}{sin(a)} = \frac{B}{sin(b)} = \frac{C}{sin(c)}\]
The formula for calculating the Square of a number is: \[v = b^2\]
Also, the formula for calculating the volume of a cube is: \[V = b^3\]
The formula for calculating the area of a rectangle is: \[v = bh\] To find slope of a line or equation, you use the following equation: \[y = mx + b\]
In addition, the formula for calculating the volume of a rectangular prism is: \[V = BH\], where \(B\), as a capital letter , is equal to \(bh\), and \(H\), also a capital letter, being the height of the three-dimensional figure.
The area of a circle is: \[A = \pi r^2\]
However, the equation for the volume of a cylinder is: \[V = \pi r^2 h\]
Furthermore, the equation for the volume of a cone is: \[V = \frac{1}{3} \pi r^2 h\]
And lastly, the volume of a sphere can be calculated with the following equation: \[V = \frac{4}{3} \pi r^3\]
